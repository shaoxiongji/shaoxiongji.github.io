Shaoxiong Ji
Independent Research Group Leader
Department of Computer Science
Technical University of Darmstadt, Germany


Shaoxiong is an independent research group leader at Technical University of Darmstadt, working on multilingual LLMs and health applications. He was a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Helsinki, working on high-performance language technology. He did his Ph.D. at Aalto University. Previously, he worked as a research assistant or visiting scholar at University of Technology Sydney (UTS), The University of Queensland (UQ), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Finnish Institute for Health and Welfare (THL), and University of Munich (LMU).
